---------WHO ARE WE?---------

Enjoy our free online courses, wherever you are and whenever you want. Access course content on mobile, tablet or desktop.

Zing Learner Evaluation & Empowerment Solutions (ZingLEES) is an exclusive evaluation partner for schools and parents who wish to get an understanding of the understanding of their children in Maths and Science in the high school across india.

Indian school assessments have majorly and traditionally focused on just evaluating the memory of the children through periodic assessment rituals. Due to various reasons we have missed out on achieving the purpose of the whole exercise of assessments. Assessments have to provide detailed diagnostics of the understanding of the learning that the learner has got. The assessments will be meaingful only when they are able to clearly evaluate the strengths, needs weakness of every learner.

With this exponentially changing global environment, where everyone is assessed or valued not based on their memory but on their ability to analyse their learning and apply them in the best suited way according to the challenges posed to them, the old assessment systems have lost relevance and have become needless.

Perfection and excellence of the next generation starts with a sincere diagnose of their current standing. Zinglees was found to fill the void in the evaluation space in indian school domain. We do mindful and purposeful evaluations.
